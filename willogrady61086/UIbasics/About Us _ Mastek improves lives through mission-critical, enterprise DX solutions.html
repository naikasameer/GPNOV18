John Owen

John Owen

Group CEO

John has held senior leadership roles in global blue-chips such as HP, Nortel and Serco, as well as successful start-ups such as Sycamore Networks. Prior to Mastek, John was the Sales and Marketing Director for Serco where his responsibilities included handling Serco’s largest customer, the UK Government.

He leads the growth strategy at Mastek and has over two decades of sales and marketing experience.

Bob King

Bob King

CEO, Americas

Based in Cambridge MA, Bob joins most recently from Oracle where he was Vice President, Oracle Commerce Consulting, a global services practice within the Retail Global Business Unit serving both the Retail and Customer Experience (CX) customer base. Prior to this role, Bob worked for a number of blue-chip technology companies such as PeopleSoft and Siebel Systems, as well as a number of successful start-ups.

Bob brings a deep passion and ambition to build a high performing culture set within the Mastek values system for our American business.

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Transforming Lives, Impacting Communities

Mastek is actively involved in initiatives that benefit our local community. We do this by aligning with charities and community activities that match our ideals.

  • Fund raising
  • Payroll Giving
  • Food Banks
  • Knowledge Sharing
  • Samaritan’s Purse
Fund raising

Our monthly sweepstakes and raffles support local charities - From estimating the weight of a yummy cake or M&M’s in large jars, to guessing the winning nation in the FIFA World Cup raffle, we have a ball, all for a good cause.

Payroll Giving

Employees take part in a salary sacrifice scheme to support NGOs on initiatives such as farmers affected by severe draught and other noble causes.

Food Banks

Our people regularly volunteer at local supermarkets on behalf of FareShare and Readifood - encouraging shoppers to donate food to support low income families in the vicinity.

Knowledge Sharing

Making use of our enterprise application technology expertise, we enable NGOs to advance their charitable initiatives – whether it is developing data repositories and BI tools or creating student attendance systems.

Samaritan’s Purse

We support the Samaritan’s Purse, Operation Christmas Child to brighten up the lives of disadvantaged kids from around the world during the festive season.
